## Unfair Insight

**What do we know that others don't?**

Most design system tools are built for large enterprises (Storybook, Figma, etc.) or require significant engineering investment. Startups and scale-ups need a design system that:

1. **Works out of the box** — No months of setup. Start shipping consistent UI in days, not weeks.
2. **Grows with the team** — Scales from 2 engineers to 20+ without breaking.
3. **Integrates with startup stacks** — Built for Next.js, React, Tailwind (not enterprise monoliths).
4. **Provides real ROI** — Reduces UI development time by 40-60% from day one.

**The unfair insight**: Startups don't need enterprise design systems. They need **startup-optimized design systems** that prioritize speed, simplicity, and immediate value over enterprise features.

**Why this matters**: Most design system tools are over-engineered for startups. They require weeks of setup, complex configurations, and enterprise features startups don't need. Startups need something that works immediately and scales naturally.

## Clear POV

**"What problem do we exist to punch in the face?"**

**The Problem**: Startups waste weeks building design systems from scratch or struggle with enterprise tools that are too complex. This slows down UI development, creates inconsistency, and burns engineering time that should be spent on product features.

**The Punch**: We exist to eliminate design system setup time and give startups a production-ready design system that works on day one, scales with the team, and integrates seamlessly with modern startup stacks.

**The Transformation**: Startups go from "we need a design system but don't have time" to "we have a design system that makes us 2x faster at shipping UI."
