## Detailed Pricing Breakdown

### 1. Limitless Guided Visualizations

**Business Model:** Freemium with one-time premium purchases

**Pricing Tiers:**
- **Free Tier:** Access to limited content
- **3 Months Premium:** $20.99 ($7.00/month)
- **6 Months Premium:** $41.99 ($7.00/month)
- **1 Year Premium:** $69.99 ($5.83/month)

**What Premium Includes:**
- Full access to all guided visualizations
- Journey Macrodoses (90+ minute immersive sessions)
- Mindset Microdoses (short guided visualizations)
- Chill Music channel (10.5+ hours of ambient music)
- Metacognitive healing methodology tools

**Additional Revenue Streams:**
- Physical products ("Intention Anchors"): $18.99-$54.64
- Gift cards available
- B2B partnerships with clinics (pricing not public)

**Pricing Strategy Insights:**
- One-time purchase (no auto-renewal) reduces friction
- Annual plan offers 17% discount vs. 3-month rate
- Positioned as affordable companion to clinical care
- Merchandising provides additional revenue
- B2B2C model means clinics may subsidize or recommend

**Why This Pricing:**
- Lower than typical therapy app subscriptions
- Accessible to patients already paying for ketamine therapy
- One-time purchase aligns with treatment course (not ongoing subscription)
- Clinic partnerships may provide volume/referral revenue

**Pricing:**

- **Clinical Services:** $2,200-$5,500+ (based on Reddit discussions)

**What's Included (Free):**
- All music and meditation content
- Educational programs
- Journey tracking and voice recording
- Community groups
- Intention setting and insight logging

**Pricing Strategy Insights:**
- App is marketing tool for Field Trip Health clinics
- Monetization happens through clinical services, not app
- Free app builds brand awareness and user base
- Users who want clinical support convert to paid services
- 4.7-star rating helps drive clinic referrals

**Why This Pricing:**
- Customer acquisition cost for clinical services is high
- Free app reduces barrier to entry
- Users experience brand before committing to expensive treatment
- Community features create lock-in and engagement
- Not sustainable as standalone app business

### 4. Wavepaths

**Business Model:** B2B SaaS subscription

**Pricing Tiers:**

**Light Plan: $30/month**
- Best for: Small private practices
- 1 user, 1 concurrent session
- All exclusive music and therapeutic functions
- Offline use, adaptive controls
- Edit and save custom templates
- On-demand education
- NO onboarding, training, remote streaming, or custom sounds

**Basic Plan: $100/month**
- Best for: Private practices or small clinics
- 1 user, 1 concurrent session
- Everything in Light, PLUS:
- 30-minute onboarding call
- Remote session streaming
- Multiple audio outputs
- Custom sounds (curated + own uploads)
- Music recommendations
- Share recordings with clients

**Pro Plan: $200+/month**
- Best for: Large clinics or research centers
- 4+ users, 4+ concurrent sessions
- Everything in Basic, PLUS:
- 60-minute onboarding call
- Staff training
- Audio setup assistance
- Session music design assistance

**Pricing Strategy Insights:**
- Professional B2B pricing (not consumer-level)
- Tiered by practice size and feature needs
- Monthly recurring revenue model
- Scalable pricing for larger organizations
- Support and training included at higher tiers
- Per-user and per-session pricing aligns with clinic usage

**Why This Pricing:**
- Reflects professional tool for therapists, not consumer app
- Pricing covers exclusive music licensing, R&D, support
- Higher tiers include white-glove service
- Clinics can bill clients for enhanced experience
- Research backing and founder credentials justify premium pricing
- Not accessible to individual consumers

### 5. Mindbloom

**Business Model:** Telehealth medical service with app component

**Pricing:**

**New Client Programs:**
- **6 Sessions:** $1,254 ($209/session)
- **12 Sessions:** $2,148 ($179/session) - Most popular
  - Payment plan: $358/month for 6 months
  - Saves $360 vs. 6-session rate
- **18 Sessions:** $2,862 ($159/session)
  - Saves $900 vs. 6-session rate

**Returning Client:**
- As low as $129/session for ongoing maintenance

**What's Included:**
- Ketamine medicine (tablets or injectables)
- 12 treatment sessions
- 4 guide coaching sessions + unlimited messaging
- 3 clinician consults
- Bloombox (physical kit)
- Mindbloom app access
- 10+ specialized programs
- Unlimited group integration circles
- Personalized care plan

**Payment Options:**
- Insurance reimbursement (save up to $1,150)
- HSA/FSA accepted
- Monthly payment plans
- No hidden fees

**Pricing Strategy Insights:**
- Premium medical service pricing
- Volume discounts incentivize larger packages
- Payment plans reduce upfront barrier
- Insurance/HSA makes it more accessible
- Positioned as "75% less" than in-clinic ($8,000+ typical)
- App is included value, not primary product
- Retention pricing for returning clients

**Why This Pricing:**
- Includes prescription medicine (regulated, expensive)
- Clinical oversight and medical team costs
- Guide coaching and support infrastructure
- Physical product (Bloombox) costs
- Research and clinical validation expenses
- Insurance reimbursement requires higher base pricing
- Competitive with other telehealth ketamine providers

**Key Insight:** Mindbloom is NOT a competitor in the app space - it's a full medical service. The app is a supporting component, not the product being sold.

## Pricing Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Price Resistance
**Concern:** Users accustomed to free apps (Field Trip, Vivid Mind)
**Mitigation:** 
- Robust free tier to demonstrate value
- Clear differentiation from free alternatives
- 7-day free trial of Premium features
- Money-back guarantee for annual plan

### Risk 2: Competition from Spotify/Generic Music
**Concern:** Users can create own playlists for free
**Mitigation:**
- Emphasize exclusive, purpose-built music
- Highlight therapeutic design and curation
- Offer features beyond music (integration, insights, community)
- Partner with artists users can't access elsewhere

### Risk 3: Market Size
**Concern:** Ketamine therapy users may be limited audience
**Mitigation:**
- Position for broader psychedelic therapy market
- Expand to meditation and breathwork users
- Target wellness and biohacking communities
- Build for growing market (psychedelic therapy expanding rapidly)

### Risk 4: Churn
**Concern:** Users may subscribe for one treatment course then cancel
**Mitigation:**
- Annual plan with significant discount
- Ongoing value through integration tools and community
- Regular content updates to maintain engagement
- Lifetime option for committed users
- Build habit through daily micro-practices

## Key Takeaways

1. **Pricing Gap Exists:** No competitor owns the $100-150/year premium consumer tier for psychedelic journey music

2. **Willingness to Pay:** Users spending $200-500/session on ketamine therapy will pay $8-15/month for quality music experience

3. **Freemium Required:** Free tier necessary to compete with Field Trip and Vivid Mind, but premium features justify paid upgrade

4. **Annual Plan Priority:** Annual subscription at $99/year should be primary revenue driver with strong incentives to convert from monthly

5. **Lifetime Option:** $299 lifetime access provides cash injection and appeals to committed psychedelic therapy users

6. **Team Plan Differentiator:** $499/year team plan uniquely positions for startup/CTO audience and creates B2B2C expansion path

7. **Value Over Price:** Focus on exclusive content, AI features, and integration tools to justify premium pricing vs. free alternatives
