## 1. Niche Overview

The target niche consists of **startup founders, Chief Technology Officers (CTOs), and Heads of Product** at early-stage and scale-up companies. This group operates in a fast-paced environment where **shipping products quickly and consistently** is a primary driver of success. They are deeply embedded in a culture of rapid growth, innovation, and resource optimization.

**Key Characteristics:**
- **Culture**: Fast-paced, agile, focused on growth and shipping velocity. Values efficiency and pragmatism over dogmatic adherence to process.
- **Identity**: Technologists, builders, and product leaders. Often former engineers who have moved into leadership roles. Entrepreneurial and comfortable with ambiguity.
- **Geography**: Concentrated in tech hubs (e.g., Silicon Valley, Toronto) but increasingly global and remote-first.
- **Values**: Efficiency, scalability, results over process, practical solutions that work on day one.
